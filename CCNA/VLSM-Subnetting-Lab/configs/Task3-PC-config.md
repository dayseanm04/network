## Step-by-Step PC Configuration

### PC1 in LAN1
1. Click on **PC1**.  
2. Go to **Desktop > IP Configuration**.  
3. Assign the **IP Address**: `192.168.5.129`  
4. Assign the **Subnet Mask**: `255.255.255.192`  
5. Assign the **Default Gateway**: `192.168.5.190`
